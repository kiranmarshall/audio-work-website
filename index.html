My Work.

I started as a runner in 2012 at the former Hackenbacker Studios (now Molinare) in Soho, London, home of shows such as Downton Abbey and Killing Eve. From making tea, I quickly graduated onto assisting lead mixer Nigel Heath, where I gained much of my sonic nous. During this time I recieved my first Emmy nomination for my contribution to Carnival's hit show Downton Abbey.
I lived in a land between analogue and digital, learning the old school principles of great sound, but utilising new technology and applying them in a new school environment to great effect.

After 3 years I decided to see what the freelance market had to offer, and was quickly connected with Howard Bargroff, head of Sonorous Post. For the following 4 years I freelanced for Howard and his associates as a Mix Tech, Reversioning Mixer, Dialogue/FX/Foley editor, and onto being a lead mixer in my own right.

In 2019 I took a year long post with Howard, where I mixed my biggest work to date alongisde him, including Doctor Who Season 12, Alex Garland's Emmy-Nominated Devs, and the upcoming Tin Star Season 3.

I am currently freelancing once again, and am looking primarily for Dialogue, FX, or Music editing roles.

Mix:
I'm totally fluent in Avid Pro Tools, for both mixing and editing. I've mixed projects all the way from Mono up to Dolby Atmos.

Delivery:
I have delivered thousands of hours of TV to production companies and broadcasters all over the world. LUFS, peaks, and folddowns: I've got it covered.

Reversioning:
I have countless days of experience creating M&E mixes for use in foreign territories, respeeding audio carefully and faithfully for use at different frame rates, and recutting and remixing existing programs for new broadcasters.

Dialogue Editing:
I am an experienced Dialogue track layer, providing a concise and clean lay to mix with. I aim to use and rescue as much production sound as possible to retain performances, and put less pressure on production and actors to record ADR.

ADR Editing:
I have also got plenty of experience fitting and delivering principal and group ADR so it sits right in with the existing lay.

Dialogue Denoising and Restoration:
I'm highly experienced at carefully and delicately restoring dialogue using the latest tools such as iZotope RX, Waves, and Cedar.

Music Editing:
I've spent many hours working closely with composers and directors on the Mix Stage, carefully editing music to shape it around Dialogue and FX, whilst retaining the composer's intentions and maximising the impact of it.

Foley Editing:
I've cut foley for both American and British TV and Film with Feet First Sound.

FX Editing:
I've provided FX lays for Short Films and some Television. It's a discipline I am very keen to gain more experience in.

Composition:
Before becoming a mixer, I scored short films, provided stings for commercial clients, and wrote music for cinematic trailers.
